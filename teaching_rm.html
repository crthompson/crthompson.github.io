Teaching

Research methods

The lectures are presented in the order they were given in. The course aimed to equip students with the basic knowledge required for them to design, carry out, analyse and write up a small-scale empirical research project.
  • Experimental vs. observational designs
  • Introduction to quantitative methods
  • Introduction to qualitative methods
  • Research ethics
  • Writing up research

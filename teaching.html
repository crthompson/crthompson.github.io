Making my slides available

While I was applying for research grants, and writing up papers after completing my PhD, I secured an Associate Teaching Staff position within the music department at the University of Edinburgh. I created a series of lectures across three modules (music neuroscience, music psychology and research methods) which were part of the "Music in the community" MSc (academic year 2011-2012). A number of people have expressed an interest in these lectures, so I decided to share the slides here. Enjoy.

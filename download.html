Thank you for showing an interest in these stimuli! To download a transcript of all the language stimuli, click here. To obtain a copy of the sound files, email me at c.featherstone(at)rcsed.ac.uk.

I hope you will find these stimuli a useful resource. If you decide to use them in your own research, please quote the following paper:

Featherstone, C.R., Waterman, M. G. and Morrison, C. M. (2012) Norming the odd: Creation, norming and validation of a stimulus set for the study of incongruities across music and language, Behavior Research Methods, 44(1), 81-94.
